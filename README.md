## Initial Problem Area

Many organizations, educational institutions, and students face challenges related to information accessibility, credential verification, and career development opportunities. Existing solutions are often fragmented, inefficient, or difficult to access. Our team is interested in exploring technology-driven solutions that improve productivity, trust, and professional growth through digital platforms.

---

## Target Users

Potential target users include:

- University students
- Educational institutions
- Employers and recruiters
- Small and medium-sized businesses (SMEs)
- Organizational employees and administrators

---

## Initial IT Venture Direction

The team is currently exploring three potential venture ideas:

### 1. AI Knowledge Management System
An AI-powered platform that enables users to search and retrieve information from organizational documents through natural language queries.

### 2. Digital Identity & Credential Verification Platform
A secure platform that allows institutions to issue, manage, and verify digital credentials and certificates.

### 3. Student Freelancer Marketplace Platform
A digital marketplace that connects students with freelance opportunities while helping them build portfolios and professional experience.

The final venture idea will be selected after opportunity analysis and validation activities in upcoming labs.

## Repository Structure

```text
project-root/
│
├── docs/
│   ├── team-profile.md
│   ├── idea-log.md
│   └── weekly-logbooks/
│
├── prototype/
│
├── data/
│
├── finance/
│
├── diagrams/
│
├── screenshots/
│
├── pitch/
│
└── README.md
```

- **docs**: Reports, profiles, idea logs, and weekly logbooks
- **prototype**: Source code or clickable prototype files
- **data**: Survey responses, validation data, and metrics
- **finance**: Financial assumptions and business model
- **diagrams**: User flow and technical architecture diagrams
- **screenshots**: Prototype and repository progress evidence
- **pitch**: Pitch deck and final presentation materials
